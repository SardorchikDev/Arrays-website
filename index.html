Dynamic Arrays

          
Arrays can change

          
let arr = [10, 20];
arr.push(69);
arr[0] = 96;

// [96, 20, 69]

          

            You can add, remove, or replace items whenever needed. Arrays are
            mutable, so they are built for changing state.
